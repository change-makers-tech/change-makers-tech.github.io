Deforestation

Forests are getting cut down and it is bad news! Please read on to find out how to save them...

What you can do?

You can use paper from managed forests. You can also help by replanting trees.

What not to do?

We should not buy paper if forests are not managed. Do not put paper in the normal bin because it can be recycled.

What Veitnam is doing (re-forestation)

Vietnam has made great progress with reforestation efforts by setting targets and planting trees to expand forest cover. A major goal is to plant 1 billion trees!
